Mission

Company founded with a mission to impart supplementary education on future technologies like Robotics, IoT, 3D printing, Artificial intelligence and in the process help students bridge the gap between yesterday' skills and tomorrow's jobs and future proof their careers.!

What we offer: Year long programs in schools, boot camps (focused short term workshops), regular after school sessions, programs in apartment communities, education centers and summer camps in various places.


We invite students to join the most exciting and innovative training modules designed to arouse the creativity of the students by providing them with a platform to build live working prototypes with the help of electronics , mechanical components, programming, circuitry, creating games and apps
